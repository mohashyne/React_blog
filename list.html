List of Prophets in Islam and Their Missions

Prophet People They Were Sent To Mission
Adam Humanity First human, taught people about Allah
Idris (Enoch) People of Babylon Preached monotheism and wisdom
Nuh (Noah) People before the flood Warned about the flood, called for repentance
Hud People of 'Ad Warned against idolatry and evil deeds
Salih People of Thamud Preached monotheism, warned against sin
Ibrahim (Abraham) People of Mesopotamia Preached monotheism, built the Kaaba
Lut (Lot) People of Sodom and Gomorrah Warned against immorality and sin
Ismail (Ishmael) Arabs of Mecca Helped build the Kaaba, preached monotheism
Ishaq (Isaac) Israelites Continued the teachings of Ibrahim
Yaqub (Jacob) Israelites Taught the message of monotheism
Yusuf (Joseph) Egyptians and Israelites Exemplified piety and forgiveness
Shu'aib (Jethro) People of Midian Warned against cheating and idolatry
Ayyub (Job) People of Hauran Exemplified patience and faith
Dhul-Kifl (Ezekiel) People of Israel Preached monotheism and righteousness
Musa (Moses) Israelites and Egyptians Delivered the Israelites, received the Torah
Harun (Aaron) Israelites Assisted Musa, preached monotheism
Dawud (David) Israelites Ruled justly, received the Psalms
Sulaiman (Solomon) Israelites and other nations Ruled with wisdom, controlled jinn
Ilyas (Elijah) People of Israel Warned against idolatry
Al-Yasa (Elisha) People of Israel Continued the work of Ilyas
Yunus (Jonah) People of Nineveh Called for repentance, exemplified mercy
Zakariya (Zechariah) Israelites Preached monotheism, father of Yahya
Yahya (John the Baptist) Israelites Preached monotheism, announced coming of Isa
Isa (Jesus) Israelites Preached monotheism, performed miracles
Muhammad Entire humanity Final prophet, delivered the Quran
